FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)



Q: When will my product arrive?

A: Within 3-5 Business days after the product has been purchased, unless otherwise bought with same day delivery.




Q: What products do you carry?

A: We carry products such as Oculus Quest 2 gear by Meta, including but not limited to glasses spacers, headsets, and controllers. We also carry Playstation VR gear and VR gear created by other companies including more known ones like Valve.




Q: How does same day delivery work?

A: Same day delivery works exactly how you expect it to, we receive the order, and one of our employees will ship it the same day after getting the message.
